03 — The Process

Five steps. Every vehicle.

Consistency is the difference between a wash and a detail. This is the sequence every car goes through, regardless of package.

01

Inspect

Full walk-around to assess paint condition, interior wear, and any problem areas before we start.

02

Decontaminate

Foam pre-wash, hand wash, and clay bar treatment to strip embedded contaminants from the paint.

03

Correct

Machine polishing removes swirl marks and light scratches, bringing back true depth and clarity.

04

Protect

Ceramic coating or sealant applied for a finish that resists dirt, water, and UV fading.

05

Finish

Interior detail, tire dressing, glass polish, and a final inspection before handoff.

Every car gets the same line — start to finish.
